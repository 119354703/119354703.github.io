Euro Disney Trip

I went to Euro Disney with my family for 4 days in 2009. It was a very exciting experience where I did all the rides including Space Mountain, the Rock 'n Roll roller coaster and the Chamber Of Horrors and I saw the Disney Parade.

France

I went on holidays to France in the Vendee Region for 2 weeks in 2011. We stayed in a campsite that was very well equipped with a swimming pool, water sports, a restaurant and lots of amenities.

Rome Trip

I went to Rome with my family and my extended family in 2015 for a four day sight-seeing trip. We saw all of the major monuments such as St.Peters Basilica, The Colosseum, The Roman Forum and the Pantheon. We ate lots of pizza, pasta and ice cream and drank lots of water as it was 35 degrees Celsius for the 4 days.

London Trip

I travelled to London in January 2019 with my English class. We went to the Globe Theatre to see a play called Macbeth which we were studying for the Leaving Certificate. We also went to Covent Gardens, where we were allowed to go shopping. It was an all around good experience and I would love to go back again.
